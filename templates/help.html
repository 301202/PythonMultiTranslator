Need Help?

Here's how to get started with LorTam:

1. How do I join a room?

Enter your name and the room code (if you have one) on the home screen, then click "Join a Room".

2. How do I create a new room?

Just enter your name and click "Create a Room". A new room code will be generated for you.

3. How does translation work?

Messages are automatically translated to the language you selected when joining.

4. Can I send files?

Yes! You can attach and send PDF files directly in the chat room.
